PREPARING A LESSON NOTE

A. General Instruction

The first page of the lesson note, at the beginning of the session

should have the following information:

1. Name of the Teacher

2. Subject

3. Class

Ideally, a note of lesson should be for one subject and for a single

class.

B. Specific Information

1. Week:

2. Date:

3. Class:

4. Subject:

5. Topic:

6. Reference Materials:

7. Instructional Materials (More than chalkboard):

8. Entry Behaviour/Previous knowledge

9. Specific Objectives: At the end of the lesson, students should be able to:

a. _____________________________

b. _____________________________

c. _____________________________

10. Content – Highlights/Summary of what you want to present.

11. Presentation (With steps)

a. Step 1 – Introduction

b. Step 2 –

c. Step 3 – (etc.)

12. Evaluation – specific questions related to your objectives should be asked, mostly questions that can be answered spontaneously.

13. Assignment.

14. Summary/Conclusion.
