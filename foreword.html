Forward

As believers in Christ, we have been called to a daily, deep, and consistent walk with God. It is such a walk that will guarantee us a place in heaven. There are many Christians who still struggle, finding it difficult to live in the way acceptable to God. Our desire and focus while on this side of eternity should be to know and understand those things needful for readiness for our home in heaven. There is no excuse for any Christian who fails in this quest.

The Bible says that we shall know the truth and the truth will make us free (John 8:32). The Bible has given us invaluable, insightful, rewarding truths that will enhance our lives as Christians as we await the glorious second coming of Jesus Christ, the Son of God. Knowing the truth contained in this manual also places upon us a responsibility â€“ the responsibility of obedience. The blessings of God are for those who hear and obey. May God give us the grace to obey Him and His word in Jesus name.

This edition of the Adult Manual for our Sunday School has been prepared by spirit-filled brethren for our spiritual well-being. It contains great lessons that will ensure our growth and development as Christians.

Beloved, let us give our devoted attention to it and allow the Holy Spirit, the Great Teacher to teach and inspire us as we go through its content. I appreciate everyone who contributed to its preparation. May God abundantly reward you all in Jesus name.

Shalom and Maranatha.

Rev. Felix Meduoye,
General Overseer,
The Foursquare Gospel Church in Nigeria
