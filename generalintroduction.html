General Introduction

Biblical Truths: It's What the Foursquare Church Believes In.

Dearly Beloved of the Lord! On behalf of the leadership of our great movement, I welcome you to the New Year, 2017. Our focus this year, shall be the studies of our doctrinal tenets, and it is believed that everyone will endeavour to be active part of our Sunday school studies as we do this only once in five years. Other seasonal lessons are also inside this manual as they are relevant.

The Holy Bible was written by men divinely inspired and is God's revelation of Himself to man. It is a perfect treasure of divine instruction. It has God for its author, salvation for its end, and truth, without any mixture of error, for its matter. Therefore, all Scripture is totally true and trustworthy. It reveals the principles by which God judges us, and therefore is, and will remain to the end of the world, the true center of Christian union, and the supreme standard by which all human conduct, creeds, and religious opinions should be tried. All Scripture is a testimony to Christ, who is Himself the focus of divine revelation.

On this truth I present to you for the next 12 months what our movement believes which is firmly standing upon the truth of the Bible as I explained above. The Foursquare Church believes that Jesus Christ is the Saviour, Baptizer with the Holy Spirit, Healer, and Soon-Coming King. We stand firm on the foundation set by Hebrews 13:8, which proclaims: "Jesus Christ the same, Yesterday and Today and Forever."

Beloved, study to show yourself approved unto God and to your students. Pray, plan, prepare and present your lessons well that your students will always look forward to your class every Sunday. God will reward you greatly. Remain blessed and be attractive with the Gospel of Christ.

Regina M. Kehinde,
National Sunday School Curriculum Minister,
The Foursquare Gospel Church in Nigeria

Our Legacy of Foursquare Doctrine

One of the distinguishing qualities for which our Foursquare Movement is renown and respected world-wide is its scripturally- balanced, solid and sound doctrines based on the unerring Word of God. This is the reason why since its inception in 1923, the Movement has not only waxed great, but is also spreading like wild fire throughout the globe.

The founder of the Movement, Aimee Semple McPherson said, “The International Church of the Foursquare Gospel is the most beautiful Gospel in the world.” It presents Jesus Christ as the four Gospels writers did. The Gospel of Luke presents Jesus as the Saviour of the world, while Apostle John, the beloved in his own gospel presents Jesus Christ as the Baptizer with the Holy Spirit. On the other hand, Mark presents Jesus as the Great never-failing Physician and Matthew presents Jesus Christ as the soon coming King.

I am very much privileged to have come across the most beautiful Gospel in the universe since 1958 and after two years became a member and a front-line worker of the about a half billion adherents of the Movement.

Our 22 tenets of faith are treated in detail to give no room for gainsayers. The Holy Scriptures are meticulously presented that there is hardly any reasonable piece of information one desires to know about the Holy Bible that our declaration of faith does not furnish. Similarly, the Eternal Godhead, our second tenet of faith was beautifully and clearly treated including the most disputable topic of Trinity. The universally accepted Athanasian Creed which is self-explicit and still stands unparalleled till the present time was quoted to make the topic very clear to any unbiased mind.

The Fall of Man and Plan of Redemption are treated as clearly depicted by the Holy Scriptures to give no man to boast of his redemption but rather to thank God. The New Birth and Daily Christian Living are treated in such graciousness that every believer should thank Jesus Christ for His precious blood shed on the cross for the eternal life we enjoy by His painful death.

The Baptism and the Lord’s Supper as well as The Baptism of the Holy Spirit are treated with constant reminiscence of the price Jesus paid for every redeemed soul. The Spirit –filled Life and The Gifts and Fruits of the Spirit, Moderation and Divine Healing are treated in our declaration of Faith to make every recipient of these virtues a debtor to Christ be grateful to Him.

The Second Coming of Christ and Church Relationship are so elaborately treated that every believer should not only keep watching but also working relentlessly for the imminent return of the glorified Jesus, making good use of the grace, gifts and talents bestowed on him or her by the Master and fulfilling their obligations to the Church of Christ on earth.

The Final Judgment speaks for itself. That the certainty of God’s judgment is inevitable; while the believers will appear before the judgment seat of Christ only to receive their rewards, Satan, his horde and the unsaved as well as Death and Hades will be judged and cast into the lake of fire. This is clearly treated in our declaration of faith.

Heaven and Hell are often spoken of on earth. There is no right-thinking person who does not believe in the two places except the atheists who deny the existence of God. To live soberly and righteously to gain Heaven and shun Hell is well treated in our tenets of Faith as well.

The Spirit of Evangelism, the 21st tenet of our faith is given an appropriate treatment in our declaration of faith. That we should “gospelize” the good news not only by our lips but also by our lives which, through the power of the Holy Spirit, draws souls to the exalted Christ is equally emphasized in our declaration of faith.

Tithes and Offerings, though the last but not the least of our tenets is well treated to enable the Church of Christ on earth carry out the last command of the risen Christ to His Church on earth. “ The last command of the risen Jesus Christ to His disciples is our first mandate on earth.”

This inspiring and heart-motivating inscription was conspicuously displayed on the Church building of one of our Churches in Oregon State- ‘To run with the command of the risen Lord, making Christ known and accepted as the only Messiah of the world calls for tithes and offerings of His children. These obligations are well taught in our declaration of faith.’

Dear brethren, it is a privilege and life span opportunity for you not only to be a member of the Foursquare Movement but also to sit at the feet of Jesus in our millions of Sunday School classes to study the Word so as to be workers who need not to be ashamed of spreading the gospel.

God bless you all in Jesus’ miracle-working name.

Rev. Dr. Gabriel O. Farombi
Pastor: Grace Pentecostal Foursquare Church
Chicago, IL. 60618
