Preface

The Lord, our Great God has in His infinite mercy made us scale through all hurdles in the last twelve months. We rejoice in His Omnipotent and Omniscient power, though not an easy task, but His grace has helped us carry out our assignment He had given us. What is written in this manual by our seasoned teachers has spoken to my heart and the Lord has ministered to me that – we need these teachings in this very circumstances of life… even as such a time as this.

I wish to acknowledge and appreciate all our writers for their scholastic contributions. To editors, for their careful proofreading and to the staff for their untiring help in typing most cases, the manuscripts and setting of the materials.

Brethren, God has called us into a life of full time Christianity. We are not only to be a full time believers, actually, our calling demand deeper Christian living embracing and expressing Biblical Truth in all facet of our existence. We need to be deeper in love, in wisdom, in knowledge of God’s word, in prayer, in evangelism, in holy living, in dependence on Holy Spirit guidance and total submission to God’s will, plan and purpose for our lives.

Dear comrades, to grow deeper in Biblical Truth, the spiritual diets provided for us in this material must be studied prayerfully. Remember, “man shall not live and be upheld and sustained by bread alone, but by every word that comes forth from the mouth of God”. (Matt. 4:4, Amplified version). Endeavour to get a copy, attend classes regularly and enjoy every bit of the teaching. Till He comes, stay blessed!

Rev. (Mrs.) K. O. Ayanbanjo
The Foursquare Gospel Church in Nigeria
