Power In Your Teaching

Teaching or service done in the energy of the flesh is of little value. The Holy Spirit's power is needed because:

1. He is the engine oil or lubricant that enhances smoothness and effectiveness in your teaching.

2. The Holy Spirit enlightens, enables and guides us as we prepare and teach.

3. The Holy Spirit's power is needed so that the Word of God may be made effective in students' lives.

4. The Holy Spirit power is essential for any proper reception of the truth (2 Cor. 2:12-15).

5. His power is needed because without Him, we can do nothing.

6. The Holy Spirit's power will remove people's spiritual blindness and regeneration will take place.

It is no gain saying therefore that both the teacher and the learner have great need of the breath of the Holy Spirit.
